Creating open source software for robots on mobile

We joined forces with Qualcomm, the global semiconductor company, and the Open Source Robotics Foundation to support the development, distribution, and adoption of open source robotics software on mobile platforms by contributing to the ROS on Android effort. Thus, offering ROS to the in new, high-end embedded platforms.

Ekumen’s team’s work has earned them a place at 2015’s ROSCon in Hamburg, Germany, to talk about their ROS Android_ndk achievements. (Link coming soon).
